Who We Are

Capturing Every Moment

Spartan Sports Media is a student-run media club at White Station High School delivering professional-level sports photography across all varsity programs. We capture the passion, dedication, and pride of Spartan athletics — from the first whistle to the final buzzer. Follow us on Instagram to see the best shots from every game.

Our Mission

Provide high-quality photo coverage of every White Station sport, distribute photos to athletes for their own personal promotion, and showcase Spartan Sports to the greater community.
